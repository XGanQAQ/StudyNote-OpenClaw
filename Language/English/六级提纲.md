# 📘 **最终可背板式提纲（你背这个直接能写作文）**

> **第一段：**  
> （现象）Nowadays…  
> （重要性）This issue is becoming increasingly important.  
> （主旨）Therefore, it is necessary to discuss why it matters.

> **第二段：**  
> First, … For example, …  
> Moreover, … As a result, …

> **第三段：**  
> In conclusion, …  
> Therefore, we should …

### 好词
- essencial
- crucial
- undoubetedly

### 开头
好用的句式
- **In modern society**, team spirit is **undoubtedly** a key component of success, **which is why** college students are increasingly encouraged to cultivate it.
	- which is 非限制性定语从句
