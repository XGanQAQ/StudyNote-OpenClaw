### **⭐ 高分范文：**

Nowadays parents are increasingly aware that allowing kids more freedom to explore and learn on their own helps foster their independence and boost their confidence. This educational philosophy is becoming more widely accepted, as society realizes that overprotection may hinder children’s long-term development.

To begin with, when children are given opportunities to make decisions and solve problems independently, they gradually learn to rely on themselves rather than adults. For example, simple tasks such as buying daily necessities or organizing their own schedule can help them build a sense of responsibility. Moreover, overcoming small challenges enables children to gain confidence, which will encourage them to face greater difficulties in the future.

However, freedom should not be equated with a lack of guidance. Children, due to their limited experience, may encounter setbacks or even risks while exploring. Therefore, parents should provide appropriate supervision and timely advice to ensure that their independence develops in a safe and meaningful way.

In conclusion, balancing freedom and guidance is essential in modern parenting. By giving children room to grow while offering necessary support, parents can help them become more confident, responsible and independent individuals.
