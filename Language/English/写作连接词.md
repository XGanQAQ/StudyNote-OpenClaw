# 英语写作常用过渡连接词

## 表时间顺序
at first, now, then, later, meanwhile, at last, in the end, before long, from then on, since then

## 表并列关系
not only...but also, both...and..., either...or..., neither...nor..., as well as

## 表因果关系
as a result, so, therefore, because of, due to, owing to, thanks to

## 表转折关系
while, but, however, yet, on the contrary

## 表解释说明
that is, that is to say, in other words, namely, such as, for example

## 表列举
first(ly), second(ly), third(ly), last(ly), in the first/second/third place

## 表递进关系
what's more, besides, in addition, worse still, moreover

## 表总结陈述
to sum up, in a word, in short, in brief, on the whole
